Story

I'm Christian. This religion changed my life so wonderfully. When I was 4 years old, my family joined The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I saw the changes come to my life through religion. It was nice to see my mom and grandma always smiling. I felt true happiness. In this picture, I met American missionaries, and I saw people with blue eyes for the first time. I thought one of them was Jack from Titanic! They were the kindest people who came to my home. I'm so grateful to them and to my religion. In the world, there are four major religions: Christianity, Islam, Buddhism, and Hinduism.

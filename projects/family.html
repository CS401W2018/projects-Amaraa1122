  • Turning Red is a 2022 American animated coming-of age fantasy comedy drama film produced by Pixar Animation Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. The film stars Rosalie Chiang as Mei Lee, a 13-year-old Chinese-Canadian student who transforms into a giant red panda when she experiences any strong emotion, due to a hereditary curse

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

A block tag takes up the entire width thus forcing a line break before and after the element. An inline element only takes up as much as the content.20

What is the margin, border, and padding?

The margin, border and padding are the properties that help define the spacing and layout of elements on a webpage.

  • Margin is the outermost space surrounding an element.
  • Border wraps around the content and padding of an element.
  • Padding is the space between an element's content and its border.
What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

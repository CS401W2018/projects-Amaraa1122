What are special entity characters?

Special entity characters in HTML are characters that have a specific meaning in the language and cannot be directly typed into the code. a pattern of characters that can represent another character in the HTML.

What are phrase tags?

Phrase tags, also known as semantic tags or inline elements, are HTML elements that define the meaning or purpose of the enclosed text.

What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

OL Tag is ordered list. We can use for creating a list of items where the order matters. The items are numbered or lettered, depending on list type. For UL Tag is unordered list, used to create a list of items where the order does not matter. Unordered list are often referred as bulleted lists. Instead numbers, each element in the list has prefixed with a special bullet symbol.
